# 🌱 GreenScape – Landscape Management System

🚀 Welcome to the GreenScape project repository!

GreenScape is a comprehensive Landscape Management System designed to streamline landscaping business operations. It efficiently handles customer management, service scheduling, inventory tracking, and employee management to ensure smooth day-to-day operations.

---

## 🏆 Project Overview

This project was developed as a group during our **second year, second semester at SLIIT**. Our goal was to build a system that enhances the management of landscaping projects and improves customer interaction through automation and smart tools.

---

## 💡 My Contributions

I was responsible for developing the **Customer Affairs Management** module, which includes:

### 🤖 AI Chatbot Integration
- Developed a chatbot to provide instant automated responses to common customer inquiries.
- Improved user experience by enabling quick support without human intervention.
- Integrated basic NLP to deliver relevant and contextual answers.

### ⭐ Feedback Management
- Implemented a customer feedback feature to collect, store, and display reviews and ratings.
- Created an interface for managers to respond to feedback to increase service transparency and trust.

---

## 💻 Technical Stack & Highlights

- **Frontend:** Responsive UI for chatbot and feedback forms using React.js.
- **Backend:** RESTful APIs built with Node.js and Express to manage feedback data and chatbot interaction.
- **Database:** MongoDB for secure storage of customer feedback and chatbot knowledge base.
- **AI Integration:** Basic Natural Language Processing logic for chatbot response generation.
- **Development Approach:** Agile methodology with continuous collaboration and sprint planning.
